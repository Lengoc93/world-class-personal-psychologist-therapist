# Relationship Letters - Therapeutic Exercise

## Overview

Write five letters to the key people who have shaped your life:
1. **Mother** - Parental relationship, family dynamics
2. **Father** - Parental relationship, family dynamics
3. **Tân** - Your ex-spouse, the marriage and divorce
4. **Vương** - Your affair, the complexity of that connection
5. **Hà** - Your current boss, leadership impact on you

**Purpose:** Process relationships, understand their impact, release emotions, integrate lessons.

**Important:** These letters are FOR YOU. You don't have to send them.

## 🎯 **What to Explore in Each Letter**

### Positioning
How did this person shape who you are and where you are now?

### Reflection
What do you understand now about this relationship that you couldn't see before?

### Lessons Learned
What are you taking forward? What are you releasing?

## 💡 **Remember**

✓ All feelings are valid - anger, love, resentment, gratitude, all of it
✓ You can feel contradictory emotions toward the same person
✓ These letters don't have to be "fair" or "balanced"
✓ Cultural guilt is normal - you can honor culture AND your truth
✓ You don't have to send these letters
✓ Take breaks - this is intense emotional work
