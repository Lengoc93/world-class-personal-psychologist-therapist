## Part 3: The Breakthrough - Underwater Screaming Discovery

### Pre-Grief Work Physical Release

Client asked whether to **run + eat out** OR **swim + order in** before grief work.

**Therapist recommended: SWIM + ORDER IN**

**Rationale:**

**Client's specific trauma = VOCAL/THROAT-BASED:**
- Scenario B revealed: "Stucking in throat"
- 33 years unable to express anger at mother (punished if spoke up)
- Unable to express needs with Tân (silenced, violated)
- Unable to express truth at work (professional mask required)

**Underwater screaming = DIRECT TARGET for stuck throat trauma**

**Why swimming > running for this client:**
1. Water holds body (safety for big emotions)
2. Can cry freely (face already wet - less shame)
3. Built-in breath regulation (prevents hyperventilation)
4. Full-body release (like running) but gentler
5. **Can scream underwater** (water muffles sound - full permission)
6. Privacy (apartment pool - no public mask needed)

**Why order in > eat out:**
- Complete control (timing, environment)
- No social mask required
- Seamless transition (swim → shower → eat → grief work)
- Can pause eating to cry if needed
