## Part 4: Reaction Formation - Understanding the Marriage's Final Phase

### What is Reaction Formation?

Defense mechanism where unconscious experiences unacceptable feeling, so conscious expresses exact opposite.

**Client's case:**
- **Unconscious:** "I'm deeply unhappy in this marriage"
- **Conscious:** "I've never loved him more! This is the best we've ever been!"

### How It Manifested

**From therapy intake (childhood doc):**
> "Bất hoà và bất mãn vô ý thức với chồng. Vì vô ý thức, nên định vị bản thân đang rất yêu chồng. Bản thân nghĩ đó là lúc yêu chồng nhất trong suốt mối quan hệ hơn 10 năm"

**The affair happened DURING reaction formation period.**

**Why dangerous:** Creates split between conscious/unconscious. Unconscious cannot be suppressed indefinitely - found outlet through Vương affair.

### Why Client Did Reaction Formation

More common in people who:
- ✓ Grew up with invalidation (Mẹ's abuse)
- ✓ Fear shame/failure ("mắc nợ xã hội" programming)
- ✓ Have anxious-avoidant attachment
- ✓ Experience cognitive dissonance ("I sacrificed Singapore for Tân" + "marriage not working" = must convince self it IS working)

### Current Confusion Explained

**Client learned feelings can LIE:**
- Felt "I love Tân most" → was reaction formation → FALSE
- Felt "I'm obsessed with Vương" → was limerence → also false but FELT more real
- Now feels "I hate Vương" but also fantasizes → **which is true?**

**Brain no longer trusts emotional data = emotional dysregulation**

### The Cognitive Trap

**Thought loop client is in:**
1. "I thought I loved Tân most during affair"
2. "But that was reaction formation (false)"
3. "Only thing that felt REAL was Vương"
4. "Therefore Vương must be real love"
5. "So why can't I get over him?"

**Error:** BOTH were false. Tân "love" = reaction formation. Vương "love" = limerence/addiction.
