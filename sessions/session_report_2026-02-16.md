**Distinction:** Biology provides DRIVE. Humans choose HOW to EXPRESS it.

**Example:** Hunger = biological drive
- Acceptable: Buy/cook food, eat politely
- Unacceptable: Steal food, hurt others to get food
- **SAME DRIVE, DIFFERENT CHOICES**

**Similarly:** Status-seeking = biological drive
- Acceptable: Achieve in career, build skills, lead effectively, earn respect through competence
- Unacceptable: Abuse spouse, control through fear, use violence, exploit power imbalance
- **SAME DRIVE, DIFFERENT CHOICES**

**Ba CHOSE abuse.** Biology gave drive for status. Biology did NOT force expression through abusing mẹ. Ba had other options (achieve through work, earn respect through competence, protect mẹ which ALSO enhances status). **Ba chose violence/control = CHOICE, not biology.**

**2. Empathy/Care ALSO Evolutionary**

**Humans are:** Social species, altricial infants (helpless for years), cooperative hunters

**Therefore empathy/care = ALSO SELECTED FOR evolutionarily.**

**Evidence:**
1. **Pair-bonding:** Males who protected mates/offspring → offspring survived better. Caring males left MORE descendants = evolutionary advantage
2. **Cooperative breeding:** Males who cooperated with partners → better child outcomes
3. **Reciprocal altruism:** Pure dominance without empathy = ostracized, killed = evolutionary DISADVANTAGE

**Conclusion:** Empathy/care is NOT "man-made illusion." Empathy/care is ALSO BIOLOGICAL. We have BOTH drives (dominance + empathy). Question is: WHICH ONE DO WE CHOOSE TO PRIORITIZE?

**Why?** Because ba CHOSE to express care through control/dominance. Ba could have ALSO chosen empathy/protection. Ba has BOTH capacities (all humans do). **Ba CHOSE ONE over other = CHOICE, not biology.**
