After showing the roadmap, ask:

1. "How does this roadmap feel? Does it still reflect what matters to you?"
2. "Are there goals here that need adjusting?"
3. "What progress are you most proud of?"
4. "Where do you feel stuck, if anywhere?"

- Add new goals
- Celebrate progress
- Revise timelines
