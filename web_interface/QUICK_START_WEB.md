# 🌸 Quick Start Guide - Web & Desktop Interface

**For using Dr. Sarah Chen via Claude.ai or Claude Desktop App**

Perfect for when you want to use **Vietnamese, English, or mixed language (Vietish)** naturally!

---

## Why Use Web/Desktop Interface?

✅ **Full multilingual support** - Vietnamese, English, Vietish all work seamlessly
✅ **Easy copy-paste** - Just attach files and paste prompt
✅ **Visual interface** - Easier to read and review conversations
✅ **Mobile friendly** - Use on phone or tablet
✅ **No technical setup** - Just upload and go

---

## 🚀 First Time Setup (2 minutes)

### Step 1: Get Your Files Ready

You need these files (they're already in your project):
- `profile/personality_assessment.json` ✓ (Updated with your latest info)
- `profile/preferences.json` ✓

These files tell Dr. Chen who you are, your personality, goals, and preferences.

### Step 2: Choose Your Access Point

**Option A: Claude.ai (Web)**
- Go to https://claude.ai
- Sign in with your account
- Click "New conversation"

**Option B: Claude Desktop App**
- Open Claude Desktop
- Click "New conversation"

---

## 📅 Daily Use

### For Full Therapy Session (20-45 min)

1. **New conversation** in Claude.ai or Desktop app
2. **Click paperclip** 📎 to attach files:
   - `profile/personality_assessment.json`
   - `profile/preferences.json`
   - (Optional) Your latest session file if you saved one
3. **Open this file**: `web_interface/THERAPY_SESSION_PROMPT.md`
4. **Copy the entire prompt** from that file
5. **Paste** into Claude chat
6. **Start talking** in Vietnamese, English, or mix them - whatever feels natural!

Dr. Chen will:
- Greet you warmly
- Do mood/energy/sleep check
- Ask probing questions
- Listen deeply and provide research-backed insights
- Help you process your day and experiences

### For Quick Daily Check (5 min)

1. **New conversation** (or reuse yesterday's check-in conversation)
2. **Open this file**: `web_interface/DAILY_CHECK_PROMPT.md`
3. **Copy and paste** the prompt
4. **Answer** the 5 quick questions
5. **Get** one micro-intervention for today

---

## 💬 Language Flexibility

### You can write:
- **Pure Vietnamese**: "Hôm nay em cảm thấy rất mệt mỏi và buồn..."
- **Pure English**: "Today I feel really tired and sad..."
- **Vietish (mixed)**: "Hôm nay em feel really overwhelmed vì có nhiều things to think about..."

### Dr. Chen will:
- Match your language choice naturally
- Switch languages if you switch
- Never judge your language mixing
- Understand cultural context in both languages

## 📁 Saving Sessions (Optional)

If you want to keep continuity between sessions:

**After each session:**
1. Ask Dr. Chen: "Can you create a session summary?"
2. Copy the summary
3. Save it to: `sessions/2025-12/session_2025-12-27.md`
4. Next time, attach this file along with your profile files

**Benefits:**
- Dr. Chen remembers previous conversations
- Track your progress over time
- Reference past insights

## 💡 Pro Tips

### For Best Experience:

1. **Use New Conversations for Sessions**
   - Keeps each session focused
   - Easier to save and track

2. **Reuse Conversations for Daily Checks**
   - Can keep one thread for the whole week
   - See your mood patterns

3. **Attach Profile Each Time**
   - Ensures Dr. Chen remembers you
   - Provides personalized support

4. **Save Important Sessions**
   - Copy summaries you want to keep
   - Attach them to future sessions for continuity

5. **Language Freedom**
   - Monday you might prefer Vietnamese
   - Tuesday might be English
   - Wednesday might be mixed
   - All totally fine!

### Common Scenarios:

**When you're emotional:**
- Vietnamese might feel more natural
- Dr. Chen understands and responds in Vietnamese

**When discussing research or concepts:**
- English might be easier for technical terms
- Dr. Chen adapts

**When thinking out loud:**
- Vietish mixing might happen naturally
- Dr. Chen follows seamlessly

## ❓ Troubleshooting

**Q: Can't attach files on mobile?**
A: Try "Request Desktop Site" in browser settings, or use desktop/laptop

**Q: Dr. Chen doesn't seem to remember me?**
A: Make sure you attached `personality_assessment.json` - this is crucial!

**Q: Response is in wrong language?**
A: Just say "Please respond in Vietnamese/English" and continue

**Q: Want to start over?**
A: Click "New conversation" and begin fresh anytime
