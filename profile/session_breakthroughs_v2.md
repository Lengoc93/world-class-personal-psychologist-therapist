- **Impact:** Released self-blame for divorce. Validated capacity for love. Hope for future relationships.

### Breakthrough 4: Body Validation — Somatic Truth

- **Insight:** "Đau nghẹn sinh lý" = somatic memory, NOT imagination. Body cannot lie about trauma.

- **Impact:** When cognitive doubt arises, body sensations serve as truth-teller.

### Breakthrough 5: Success DOES NOT Negate Trauma

- **Paradox resolved:** "Em successful VÀ em bị abuse — HAI ĐIỀU ĐỀU ĐÚNG ĐỒNG THỜI"

### Breakthrough 6: Mother Boundary Clarity
